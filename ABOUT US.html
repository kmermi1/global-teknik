ABOUT US

 

Global Teknik was established in 2000 to serve all modern industry establishment needs from the beginning to functioning state of a factory.

 

Global Teknik presents its own solutions successfully for Electric, Electronic, SCADA, and PLC Automation, Mechanic, System Designing, Projects, Technical Assistance, Training, Providing Parts.

 

As it could be seen by its references, Global Teknik successfully actualized modern industry establishment functions of lots of sectors besides beverage sector not domestically but also internationally.

 

Global Teknik completed a lot of projects in GERMANY, FRANCE, SWISS, MACEDONIA, MALTA, ALGERIA, and TURKEY so far.

 

Due to increasing demands, Global Teknik opened its sister company with the same name in Algeria and completed its organization in Algeria.

 

Global Teknik growing day by day will continue to its works in the global market.
